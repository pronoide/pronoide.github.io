What are we looking for?

We are looking for technical trainers for at least one of the following profiles:

Java & Spring Trainer

Spring, Spring boot, Microservices,
Groovy, Gradle, Jenkins, Sonar, etc.

Wen & JavaScript Trainer

TypeScript, Node, Angular, HTML,
CSS, Webpack, React, VUE, etc.

DevOps Trainer

Linux, Servidores Aplicaciones, Jenkins,
Sonar, Docker, Kubernetes, OpenShift, etc.

Big Data & NoSQL Trainer

MongoDB, Cassandra, Hadoop,
Spark, Elasticsearch, Kibana, etc.

What do we offer?

  • Possibility to learn constantly new technologies.
  • A nice and collaborative work environment.
  • Obligation to attend your courses (the typical course is usually about 25 hours per week), but the rest of the time is managed by yourself.
  • A lot of holidays, long weekends, and slow weeks (rhythm of work of low intensity).
  • Contract of indefinite duration and salary of € 30,000.

How are we?

To give you an idea of how we are, this is our Ideology:
  • People are worth than money. We treat people fairly.
  • We are passionate about technology and innovation. Work is fun.
  • We are a team, your problems are our problems.
  • The problems, the sooner the better.
  • Major changes can never be urgent, but must be done consistently and step by step.
  • We don't want heroes, efforts are timely.
  • Teach helps us to learn, and... there's so much to learn!
  • Sharing our knowledge is a way to change the world in which we live.
  • We help improve the professional lives of our students.
  • Be transparent, legal and honored allows us to demand the same from our colleagues, students, clients, suppliers and society.

What are we ask for?

  • Technology passion: people with enthusiasm and motivation to learn new stuffs and become expert in their expertise area.
  • Ability to self-manage his own time and to learn by himself.
  • Ability to have flexible working hours: there are courses in the morning and other courses in the afternoon.
  • Ability to travel throughout Spain (usually one week per month).
  • Ideally fluent in English.

Are you interested?

  • If you are interested in this job offer and you want to work on the Pronoide's staff we would like to see you "in action".
  • Record yourself on a video explaining a technical subject that you know well (4-5 minutes).
  • Also record a session of "Live Coding" explaining and creating a small component or a mini application (4-5 minutes).
  • Use any screen recorder to record your videos. What matters is not video production but seeing you programming and explaining technical issues.
  • Upload the videos in "private mode" to youtube and send us the links with your CV to: crotari@pronoide.es.
